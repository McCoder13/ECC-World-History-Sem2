Israel

God Delivers Israel

Israel had been in Egypt for hundreds of years, many of them under heavy slavery. They were desperately wanting to escape Egypt. Then God raised up Moses, to lead Israel out of Egypt. God struck Egypt with ten plagues before the Pharaoh decided to let Israel go.

Over the next forty years, God led Israel to the promised land. Along the way, God saves Israel several times and provides food and water through many miracles.
