Greek Civilization

Overview of Greek History

Greece can be traced back to the Minoan and Mycenaean civilizations. They are thought to have been displaced and moved to the Greek peninsula. Greece developed itself into city-states, several of the large ones being Athens and Sparta. City-states would often war with each other. Greece is known for its architecturally massive structures and recording of history. Many famous Greek philosophers came up with ground-breaking discoveries in math and science. Greeks were pagan, worshiping many gods and completely rejecting God. Greece still has a major impact on the world today as one of the most infulencial civilizations of all time.
