Before you start...

When you click that button below, you will be scrolling through animations, short paragraphs, and images presenting the early histroy of our world. Spanning from Creation all the way to the Middle Ages, this will cover various empires, influencial persons, and important events. It's not a step by step overview of each and every empire, person, or event, rather it will jump from various empires, persons, and events navigating through history until around the late Middle Ages.
