Early Civilizations

Ancient civilizations were advanced

Contrary to what most "history" books portray, there is no archaeological evidence of cave dwellers giving way to man-made housing. What archaeological evidence (and biblical evidence) shows is nomads existed at the same time as settlements.

Early on, humans created governments, economies, and social systems. The earliest known civilizations show that people were creating, thinking, and advancing from the very beginning.
