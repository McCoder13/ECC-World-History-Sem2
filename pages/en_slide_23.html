Age of Scientific Revolution

Some of the key figures associated with the scientific revolution include Galileo Galilei, Johannes Kepler, and Isaac Newton. Their contributions helped to establish a new approach to science that emphasized observation, experimentation, and the use of mathematics and quantitative methods to analyze data.

The scientific revolution marked a major turning point in the history of science, paving the way for many of the advances and discoveries that have shaped our modern world. But also leading the vast majority of scientists to disregard the true Creator of the Universe, and open the question they can never answer, what is our purpose?
